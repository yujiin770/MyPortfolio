Meet The Characters

The heroes and villains at the center of the adventure

Protagonist

The Time Guardian

A naturally curious student who accidentally triggers a time-traveling artifact. Joined by a seasoned explorer from another era, they must combine their wits and agility to run through history, collect scattered artifacts, and restore the timeline before it's too late.

See Gameplay
Antagonist

The Eraser

A mysterious entity born from corrupted data and historical inaccuracies. The Eraser seeks to unravel the timeline by consuming historical artifacts, believing that a blank slate is preferable to a flawed history. It is a relentless pursuer, manifesting as a glitchy, ever-changing figure that embodies the very essence of chaos.

HOW TO PLAY

MASTER THE DASH

Use intuitive swipe controls to survive. Swipe UP to jump over barriers, DOWN to slide under obstacles, and LEFT or RIGHT to switch lanes. Tap the screen to activate powerful artifacts you collect along the way!
