About the Game

Insight of the Game

Martin is an ordinary man who never imagined he'd be forced to survive in a world ravaged by a zombie apocalypse. A former high school teacher, his life is turned upside down when a viral outbreak turns most of humanity into mindless, flesh-eating zombies.

Objective

To create a sense of tension, fear, and immersion, pulling players into a world where they feel vulnerable and under threat. The objective is to engage players emotionally, making them feel part of the terrifying world they’re in.

How To Play

Use the arrow keys for movement (Up, down, left and right) and Press the Spacebar to shoot. Do your best to survive the thriller nights without getting infected. Avoid the undead and kill them before they can bite you!

Conclusion

A successful zombie horror game combines all these elements to create a gripping and rewarding experience that resonates with players long after the game is over, keeping them coming back for more.

Core Features

Player Control

- For movement, we will utilize the standard Left, Up, Down, Right arrow keys and space for shooting like in other RPG games.

Graphics and Visuals

We want to create the game more understandable just by using Visual Basic 2010 as much as possible, with top-down view interface and camera angles, so that players may feel the peak of the horror/survival genre.
